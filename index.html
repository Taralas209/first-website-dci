Kazakhstan

Discover My Homeland

Geography

Kazakhstan is the world’s largest landlocked country, situated in the heart of Eurasia and is the ninth-largest country in the world, with a land area about four times the size of Germany. It shares borders with Russia, China, Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan, and Turkmenistan, and its diverse terrain ranges from high mountain ranges to vast steppes and deserts.

Nature

Kazakhstan offers a diverse range of landscapes, from majestic mountains and serene lakes to vast steppes and deserts. The country is home to lush valleys, high-altitude plateaus, and pristine alpine lakes. In the south, the towering Tian Shan mountains house ancient glaciers and extensive coniferous forests, providing refuge for the unique and diverse fauna of the region, including the elusive snow leopard.

Attractions

Kazakhstan is home to a myriad of landmarks and attractions that reflect its rich history, diverse ecology, and unique culture. From the significant Baikonur Cosmodrome and the stunning Charyn Canyon to the delightful culinary traditions, every attraction offers a unique glimpse into the country’s heritage. Explore the highlights below to discover the remarkable diversity and charm of Kazakhstan.

Baikonur Cosmodrome

The Baikonur Cosmodrome is a symbol of Kazakhstan's significant contributions to space exploration. It has been the launch site for many historic space missions, including the launch of the first human, Yuri Gagarin, into space.

Charyn Canyon

Charyn Canyon is a natural wonder of Kazakhstan, known for its stunning rock formations and diverse ecosystems. It's often compared to the Grand Canyon for its impressive geological features and scenic beauty.

Kazakh Cuisine

Kazakh cuisine reflects the rich cultural and nomadic heritage of the country. It's characterized by hearty and flavorful dishes, predominantly featuring meat, dairy, and grains, such as Beshbarmak and Kumis.

Big Almaty Lake

Big Almaty Lake is a picturesque mountain lake surrounded by majestic peaks and alpine meadows, serving as a popular spot for relaxation and tourism.

Altyn-Emel

Altyn-Emel National Park is renowned for its unique natural features like the Singing Dunes and the colorful Aktau mountains, showcasing the ecological diversity and beauty of Kazakhstan.

Singing Sand Dune

The Singing Sand Dune is a mesmerizing natural phenomenon. When the wind sweeps across the dune, it produces melodic, organ-like sounds, giving the dune its musical name.
